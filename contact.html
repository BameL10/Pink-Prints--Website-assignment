Does Pink Prints only sell pink items

Yes!Our main focus is to deliver pink items to queens who believe in the power of pink

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International orders typically take 7-14 business days to arrive.

Can I visit your boutique in person?

Absolutely! We'd love to see you at 123 PTA Avenue, Dennis City. Check our operating hours above.

How do I care for my pink garments?

We recommend hand washing in cold water with similar colors. Lay flat to dry to maintain that perfect pink hue!
