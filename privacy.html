Privacy Policy

At PinkPrints, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit or make a purchase from our site.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Browsing and purchase history

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us to:

  • Process orders and manage deliveries
  • Send order updates and confirmations
  • Provide customer support
  • Personalize your shopping experience
  • Send newsletters and promotions (if you subscribe)

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience on our site. Cookies help us remember your preferences and monitor site performance. You may disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share information with trusted providers who assist us in operating our website and processing transactions, such as payment gateways and shipping partners.

5. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information, including secure servers, encryption, and regular security reviews.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletion
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@pinkprints.com.
