Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I place an order?

Simply browse our collections, click on the item you love, select your size, and click "Add to Cart." When you're ready, go to your cart and follow the checkout steps.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept GCash, credit/debit cards, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely for your protection.

3. Can I change or cancel my order?

If your order hasn’t been processed yet, you may change or cancel it by contacting us immediately at orders@pinkprints.com. Once shipped, we can no longer make changes.

4. What are your delivery options?

We offer standard delivery via our partner couriers. Delivery takes 3–5 business days after dispatch. You will receive a tracking link once your package is on its way.

5. Do you offer refunds or exchanges?

Yes, we offer refunds and exchanges for items returned within 14 days in original condition. See our Shipping & Returns policy for full details.

6. My item arrived damaged or incorrect. What should I do?

We sincerely apologize! Please email us within 3 days of receiving your order at help@pinkprints.com with a photo of the item and your order number. We'll make it right.

7. Do I need an account to shop?

No, you can check out as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to save your information and view your order history.

8. Where are you located?

We are proudly based in the Philippines. Our fashion is inspired by local trends and the beauty of all things pink!

Still have questions? Contact us at support@pinkprints.com.
