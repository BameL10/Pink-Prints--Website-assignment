Shipping & Returns

Shipping Information

We deliver across Botswana! Once your order is placed, please allow 1–2 business days for processing. Standard delivery times range from 3–5 business days depending on your location.

Delivery Fees

Shipping is free for orders over P500. For orders under P500, a flat shipping fee of P60 applies. All delivery fees will be displayed at checkout.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number via email or SMS so you can follow its journey to your doorstep.

International Shipping

Currently, PinkPrints ships around the whole world . Stay tuned — we’re working on expanding internationally soon!

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love what you ordered! If you're not completely happy with your purchase, you may return it within 14 days of delivery.

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags.
  • To request a return, please contact us at returns@pinkprints.com.
  • Return shipping fees are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was defective or incorrect.

Refunds

Once we receive your return and confirm its condition, a refund will be processed within 7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Need Help?

For questions about shipping, delivery, or returns, email us at support@pinkprints.com or visit our FAQs page.
