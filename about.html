Our Pink Story

Where pink isn't just a color - it's confidence, power, and unapologetic self-expression

How the brand started

PinkPrints is a brand started by a girl who grew up as a lover of all things pink and cute and wore it as a way of showing her true personality and who she aspires to be in the future. She made sure to buy and dress in pink for any occasion as a way of showing people that you can always grow up and still stick to one color because it is said that you never get tired of what you truly love.

She then put it upon herself to create a brand to inspire other women, ladies, teen and kids to be able to wear pink and embrace their unique qualities, own their power and use it to express themselves too.
